# DeFi Total Value Locked Surpasses $100 Billion

**Source:** The Block  
**Date:** January 14, 2024

The decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystem has reached a significant milestone, with Total Value Locked (TVL) crossing $100 billion for the first time since May 2022.

## Market Analysis

The DeFi sector has shown remarkable resilience and growth:

- **Ethereum** remains the dominant chain with $65 billion TVL
- **Layer 2 solutions** are gaining traction, with Arbitrum and Optimism combined reaching $8 billion
- **Yield farming** activities have increased by 30% month-over-month

## Key Protocols

Leading protocols by TVL:

1. Lido Finance: $12.5 billion
2. Aave: $8.2 billion
3. MakerDAO: $7.8 billion
4. Uniswap: $6.5 billion

The growth is driven by improved user experience, lower transaction costs on Layer 2s, and innovative yield strategies that attract both retail and institutional participants.
